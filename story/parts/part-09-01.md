A faded map unfolded on the holosurface, its coordinates misaligned with current charts. Sam frowned as he traced the route, piecing together a pattern that hinted at deliberate erasure. He made a list of anomalies and tucked it into the secure queue; if someone was manipulating archival coordinates, it could explain other disappearances they were seeing. He looked over his shoulder and closed the map. In that small act he acknowledged the presence of others' eyes and the need to move carefully.
